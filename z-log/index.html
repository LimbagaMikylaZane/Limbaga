IT 111 Blog Entries

What I learned

This year, I just learned that understanding computing illuminates deep insights and questions into the nature of our minds, our culture, and our universe. The subject taught us a lot of lessons and how valuable computing is, especially in this generation since the world runs with technology. Depend on how we use technology, it holds great benefits, and brings powerful impact to mankind despite the flaws and negative impacts it has.

My Favorite Lesson

My favorite lesson for this subject so far is the Basic Concept of Computer Security. I believe that the reason why this is my favorite one is that it is beneficial to me and the others, since today's generation has more risk being exposed to hackers and other attackers online. Knowing a little about how to prevent inconveniences that may happen to me is an important, and might also be, a necessary knowledge to have.

A Challenging Lesson

Rating the difficulty of learning the Number Systems, I would say 7/10. For me, it's fun to learn how to get binary codes, decimals and etc. but sometimes it gets confusing and as the lesson progress, it gets harder and harder. Despite the difficulty of this lesson, I gained a little knowledge and understanding the nature of mathematics, and how valuable understanding this lesson to programmers in order to manipulate data efficiently and effectively.
